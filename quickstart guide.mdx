## Getting Started

Welcome to Hardware FYI Pro! Congratulations on taking this step towards reaching your dreams as a mechanical engineer.

The purpose of this program is not for you to log on and read a few of the pages, only to never sign back on in a few weeks.

<Check>
This program was designed to take you from ground zero to getting an internship/full-time job in 2-3 months.
</Check>

It’s going to take active effort and commitment. But, to make your life easier and save you as much time as possible, we’ve designed this course to contain **the most useful and relevant information for recruitment.**

We’ve scoured textbooks, our own past interview notes, YouTube videos, online resources, and more to distill the most necessary, useful information into this one-stop-shop.

## Outline

Hardware FYI Pro consists of three sections written to take you from 0 to 1.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="1. Interviewing">
    ☝️ The **first part** provides an overview to the hardware engineering interview process: what types of roles are available, the skills required, and ways to gain the experience needed.
    <Tip>
    We’ll teach you how to gain experience, write rock-star resumes, design portfolios, and how to hack your way into landing interviews.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="2. Technical Interview Prep.">

    The **second part** consists of technical interviewing preparation, and explains the interviewer’s mindset and the traits they’re looking for in a candidate. This part does a deep dive in the most common technical interviewing questions paired with comprehensive solutions.
    <Tip>
    We’ll outline the interviewing process & cover the engineering fundamentals you’ll need to know from our 8+ collective years of interviewing at top companies.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="ME Fundamentals">
    The **third part** will teach you all about mechanical engineering fundamentals & industry-knowledge not often taught in academic settings. Get the killer advantage over other candidates with what we'll teach you.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
